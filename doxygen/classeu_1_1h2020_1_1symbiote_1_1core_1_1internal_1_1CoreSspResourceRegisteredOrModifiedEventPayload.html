Detailed Description

Extension of the CoreResourceRegisteredOrModifiedEventPayload for the Ssp resources. CoreResourceRegisteredOrModifiedEventPayload#platformId is identifying symbIoTe cloud entity, so it should be equal to ssp's id. The sdev whose resources are being registered is represented by sdevId;
